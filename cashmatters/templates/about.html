About Cash Matters

Cash Matters is a global platform, supported by the International Currency Association (ICA), dedicated to promoting the role and relevance of cash as an essential part of the payment landscape, both now and in the future.

Our Mission

Cash Matters highlights the importance of cash as a foundation of democracy, personal freedom, and independence. It works in close cooperation with other organizations in the cash sector to maximize its global reach and impact.

The Cash Matters website serves as a comprehensive resource, offering a library of articles, studies on key cash-related issues, videos aimed at the general public, and a strong presence across major social media channels. These resources are designed for industry professionals, decision-makers, and the wider public, making information about the significance of cash easily accessible.

What We Do

Research

We conduct comprehensive research on cash usage patterns, economic impacts, and consumer preferences worldwide to inform policy and business decisions.

Advocacy

We advocate for policies that protect payment choice and ensure that cash remains a fundamental component of modern economies.

Education

We educate businesses, policymakers, and consumers about the importance of cash and its role in creating inclusive financial systems.
