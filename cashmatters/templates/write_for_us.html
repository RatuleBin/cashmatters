We Want to Hear From You

Cash Matters welcomes contributions from writers, researchers, economists, and advocates who share our commitment to preserving payment choice. Whether you're an academic, industry expert, or passionate advocate, we'd love to hear your perspective.

We're looking for well-researched articles, opinion pieces, and stories that explore the importance of cash in today's society. Topics can include financial inclusion, privacy, economic resilience, consumer rights, and the future of payments.

What We're Looking For

  • Original, unpublished content related to cash and payments
  • Well-researched articles with credible sources
  • Diverse perspectives on financial inclusion and payment choice
  • Personal stories about the importance of cash access
  • Expert analysis on policy and economic trends

Submission Guidelines

  • Articles should be between 800-2000 words
  • Include a brief author bio (50-100 words)
  • Provide high-quality images if available
  • Cite all sources and statistics
