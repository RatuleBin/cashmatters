About Cash Matters

We are an independent initiative dedicated to preserving payment choice for everyone. Our mission is to ensure that physical cash remains a viable and accessible payment option in an increasingly digital world.

Learn Our Mission

Our Mission

Cash Matters believes that everyone should have the freedom to choose how they pay. We advocate for the preservation of physical cash as an essential public good that promotes financial inclusion, privacy, and economic resilience.

In a world increasingly dominated by digital payments, we work to ensure that cash remains accessible, accepted, and valued by businesses, governments, and communities worldwide.

What We Do

Our work spans research, advocacy,
and community engagement

Research

We conduct comprehensive research on cash usage patterns, economic impacts, and consumer preferences worldwide to inform policy and business decisions.

Advocacy

We advocate for policies that protect payment choice and ensure that cash remains a fundamental component of modern economies.

Education

We educate businesses, policymakers, and consumers about the importance of cash and its role in creating inclusive financial systems.

Why Cash Matters

Cash is more than just a payment method—it's a fundamental right and a critical component of a resilient economy. It provides:

Financial Inclusion

Accessible to everyone, regardless of age, income, or technological literacy.

Privacy & Security

Protects personal data and provides transaction anonymity.

System Resilience

Works during power outages, network failures, and cyber attacks.

Budget Control

Helps with financial discipline and tangible spending awareness.

Our Values

The principles that guide our work and advocacy

Inclusion

We believe payment systems should serve everyone—from the elderly to the underbanked, from rural communities to urban centers. Cash ensures no one is left behind in our financial ecosystem.

Choice

We advocate for consumer freedom—the right to choose how to pay without coercion or limitation by governments or corporations. Cash represents true payment sovereignty.

Resilience

We champion robust payment systems that work in all circumstances—during emergencies, infrastructure failures, and economic crises. Cash is the ultimate backup system.
